AI SOCIAL Research
What are the consequences?
We are striving to develop artificial intelligence around the planet. It has been portrayed as a race or a fotball match between nations. Meanwhile machine learning has already been implemented across many sectors. Let us be critical, interested and inquisitive.
To be cutting edge you need to know
the edge which you cut
Working together social scientists with different backgrounds can begin to ask important questions together with computer scientist or through learning programming. Codes both written and unwritten needs to be questioned as society implements new technology. We need social scientists who engage with advanced programming and we need different computer engineers with a keen interest for social science.
