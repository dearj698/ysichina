AI Social Research was initiated at the Social Sciences Faculty at the University of Oslo on the 23rd of January 2019. It was started by an interdisciplinary team of social scientists both students and recent graduates. The research group was initiated to facilitate a greater understanding of the consequences and possible impact of artificial intelligence in society. Our current open meeting times are as follows:

27th of February at 18.00 - Social Sciences Faculty (Eilert Sundt), Oslo
20th of March at 18.00 - Social Sciences Faculty (Eilert Sundt), Oslo
17th of April at 18.00 - Social Sciences Faculty (Eilert Sundt), Oslo

If you are elsewhere you can still participate in our Facebook group and we will do our best to share any notes or reflections made on each occasion. If you want to organise some meetings of your own in your own location please post about it in the group right here:
https://www.facebook.com/groups/aisocialresearch/
Programming in R weekly @ Oslo
One of our members run a weekly programming session where you can meet up and do programming in R focused on the social sciences at the Faculty of Social Science in the University of Oslo (UiO). The session runs from 17.30-19.00 in Eilert Sundts house. The focus for most of the group may be related to a module at our university called STV1515 - Machine Learning and Programming for the Social Sciences. The subject is related to Political Science and relatively small datasets. However you may focus on other interests or projects if you prefer.
